# OSBR Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-15 | Last updated: 2026-04-15

[[TOC]]

## 1. Privacy Statement

OSBR ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of individuals who interact with our company — whether as a client, team member, job candidate, website visitor, service user, or business partner. We handle personal data in accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA, Act 709) and design our systems with data minimisation in mind.

This policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information across OSBR's operations — including client engagements, employment, recruitment, our public websites, our software tools, and business communications.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly process personal data of minors.

For more information about OSBR, visit [https://www.osbrjp.com](https://www.osbrjp.com).

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All OSBR business operations, including web development, design, and consulting engagements.
- All OSBR software tools and services.
- All OSBR websites, including the company handbook.
- All individuals whose personal data OSBR processes, including clients, team members, job candidates, website visitors, service users, and vendors or partners.

Where a specific engagement or employment agreement contains additional privacy terms, those terms supplement this policy.

OSBR processes all personal data in accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA, Act 709), including the seven data protection principles established in Sections 6 through 12 of the Act.

For detailed, principle-by-principle documentation — including how each principle is implemented across all data categories — see [Legal Compliance (PDPA 2010)](./privacy-pdpa).

## 6. Google API Services Limited Use Disclosure

OSBR's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

- We only use Google API data to provide and improve the user-facing features you interact with.
- We do not transfer Google API data to third parties unless necessary to provide the service, required by law, or with your explicit consent.
- We do not use Google API data for advertising or to build user profiles.
- We do not allow humans to read Google API data except with your consent, for security purposes, to comply with the law, or when data has been aggregated and anonymised for internal operations.

## 7. Data Storage and Retention

| Data type | Retention | Basis |
| --------- | --------- | ----- |
| Client and project data | As specified in engagement agreement, subject to applicable law | Contractual, Companies Act 2016 |
| Team member records | Duration of employment plus period required by law | Employment Act 1955 |
| Payroll and tax records | Duration of employment plus 7 years | Income Tax Act 1967 |

| Business communications | As required for business operations and applicable law | Business necessity |

When retention periods expire, data is deleted or irreversibly anonymised in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

- **Access**: Request information about the personal data we hold relating to you.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal retention requirements.
- **Withdrawal of consent**: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.
- **Data portability**: Where applicable under the PDPA Amendment Act 2024, request that your personal data be transmitted to another data controller.
- **Revoke Google access**: Remove OSBR's access to your Google account through your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
- **Opt out of analytics**: Prevent Google Analytics tracking using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or your browser's cookie settings.
- **Lodge a complaint**: File a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commissioner of Malaysia.

**How to exercise your rights**: Email info@osbrjp.com with the subject line "Personal Data Request." We will respond within 21 days as prescribed by Section 12(2) of the PDPA. See [Legal Compliance — Access Principle](./privacy-pdpa#_7-access-principle-section-12) for the full process.

## 9. Security

OSBR implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, in accordance with Section 9 of the PDPA.

For details on security measures by data category, see [Legal Compliance — Security Principle](./privacy-pdpa#_4-security-principle-section-9).

## 10. International Data Transfers

OSBR software services are hosted on Google Cloud Platform. Personal data may be processed on servers located outside Malaysia. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with Section 129 of the PDPA.

| Third party | Role | Data involved |
| ----------- | ---- | ------------- |
| Google Cloud Platform | Application hosting and server logging | Authentication logs, server access logs, OAuth tokens (in memory) |
| Google Analytics | Website analytics for the handbook site | Anonymised visitor data |

## 11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, OSBR will investigate the breach, take steps to contain it, notify affected individuals without unreasonable delay, and report to the Personal Data Protection Commissioner within the timeframe prescribed by applicable law. See [Legal Compliance — Data Breach Notification](./privacy-pdpa#_10-data-breach-notification-pdpa-amendment-act-2024) for the statutory framework.
